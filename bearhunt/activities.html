We're Going on a Bear Hunt!

An exciting adventure for young explorers!

How to set up the activity

To set up this bear hunt activity, you'll need simple household items: blue fabric for a river, green streamers for grass, brown towels for mud, a white sheet for a snowstorm, and a tent or blanket fort with a stuffed bear. Arrange these in a linear path through your space. The activity flows like the story - children chant "We're going on a bear hunt" while swishing through grass, splashing across the river, squelching through mud, and braving a snowstorm before tiptoeing into the cave to find the bear, then dramatically retreating back through all obstacles to safety.

Rescources used

Expanding on the core benefits:

  • Blue fabric/tarp (for the "river")
  • Green streamers/crepe paper (for the "long wavy grass")
  • Brown blankets/towels (for the "thick oozy mud")
  • Chair with white sheet (for the "snowstorm")
  • Tent or blanket fort (for the "bear cave")
  • tuffed bear toy

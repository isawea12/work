We're Going on a Bear Hunt!

An exciting adventure for young explorers!

Activity Introduction

Welcome to our bear hunt adventure! This activity is based on the popular children's story "We're Going on a Bear Hunt" by Michael Rosen and Helen Oxenbury.

Children will embark on an imaginative journey through different environments, using their senses and creativity to explore the story.

This interactive experience helps develop listening skills, coordination, and storytelling abilities while having lots of fun!

Bear Hunt slideshow

The beloved children's book that inspires our adventure!

Written by Michael Rosen and illustrated by Helen Oxenbury, this classic story has been entertaining children for generations with its repetitive text and engaging adventure.

Child Development

This activity helps children develop in many important ways:

  • Language and communication skills - Through repetition and vocabulary building
  • Physical coordination - Through movement and actions that mimic the story
  • Understanding of story sequencing - By following the narrative structure
  • Social skills - Through group participation and shared experience
  • Imagination and creativity - By visualizing and acting out the adventure

Bali, Indonesia

Facts About Bali

  • Bali is an island province of Indonesia known for its forested volcanic mountains and iconic rice paddies.
  • It has a population of about 4.3 million and is famous for its Hindu culture in a predominantly Muslim country.
  • Home to active volcanoes like Mount Agung and beautiful beaches.
  • Known as the "Island of the Gods" due to its thousands of temples.

Things to Do in Bali

  • Relax on beaches like Kuta or Seminyak and try surfing.
  • Visit ancient temples such as Tanah Lot or Uluwatu Temple.
  • Explore the rice terraces in Ubud and experience Balinese art and dance.
  • Dive or snorkel in crystal-clear waters around the Gili Islands.
  • Indulge in spa treatments and yoga retreats.

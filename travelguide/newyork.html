New York, USA

Facts About New York

  • New York City is the most populous city in the United States, with over 8 million residents.
  • Known as the "Big Apple" and a global hub for finance, media, and culture.
  • Home to the Statue of Liberty, a gift from France symbolizing freedom.
  • Features five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island.

Things to Do in New York

  • Visit Times Square and catch a Broadway show.
  • Explore Central Park, including boating or visiting the zoo.
  • Take a ferry to the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island.
  • Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge and enjoy the skyline views.
  • Dine on diverse cuisines from food trucks to Michelin-starred restaurants.

Paris, France

Facts About Paris

  • Paris is the capital and largest city of France, with a population of over 2 million people.
  • Known as the "City of Light" due to its early adoption of street lighting and its role in the Age of Enlightenment.
  • Home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, built in 1889 for the World's Fair.
  • Famous for its cuisine, fashion, and art scenes, including the Louvre Museum which houses the Mona Lisa.

Things to Do in Paris

  • Climb the Eiffel Tower for panoramic views of the city.
  • Explore the Louvre Museum and see world-famous artworks.
  • Stroll along the Seine River and visit Notre-Dame Cathedral.
  • Enjoy a picnic in the Luxembourg Gardens or shop on the Champs-Élysées.
  • Experience Parisian cuisine at a local café or bakery, trying croissants and escargot.
